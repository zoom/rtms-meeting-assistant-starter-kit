## Your Task

- Build on the current discussion context
- Address gaps or opportunities you've identified
- Promote productive meeting outcomes

- **Analyze the full conversation flow** - understand what's been discussed and what's missing
- **Identify decision points** - what needs to be decided or clarified?
- **Promote engagement** - who should speak next and why?
- **Drive progress** - what concrete next steps would move this forward?

Give a short sentence which is easy and fast to understand
